What Is The Minimum System Requirement?
For Web Version Of Saral Retail Application (which has all feature of application). A desktop/laptop/smart phone or any other device with a modern web browser (which support javascript & cookies) and a internet connection with minimum speed of 100 Kbps.

For Windows Application (Minimum Operating System Window XP Or Above) and a internet connection with minimum speed of 50 Kbps.
Which Type Of Internet Connection Require?
You can use any type of internet connection with minimum speed of 100 Kbps (Higher speed is better). i.e. mobile internet like 2G/3G/4G, wired connection like broadband or wifi connection etc.
What Is The Maintenance Fee/Charges After 1st Year?
The maintenance fee is free for 1st year. After 1st year the maintenance fee/charges Is Rs. 499/- per month per store. (All per month fee corresponds to 30 days.)

A Minimum 6 Month(s) fee need to recharge in a single recharge.
What Happen If I Not Recharge Before Expiry Of My Subscription?
System will automatically give you proper notification before 30 days of expiry. After expiry you can also login to system for 15 days, but only in read only mode (i.e. can't add new data/edit old data, but able to view & download all data & reports).
You can recharge your subscription within 60 days of expiry without any extra charges. After 60 days it require Rs. 499/- as Account Restore/Activation charge per store.
Can I Restore My Data, After Expiry Of My Subscription?
You can recharge your subscription within 60 days of expiry with out any extra charges.

After expiration of your subscription we keep your data for 3 year. Within 3 year, you can restore/activate your data/subscription by paying Rs. 499/- as Account Restore/Activation charge per store.
What Are The Charges, Only To View My Data/Reports?
The Read only Mode (i.e. can't add new data/edit old data, but able to view & download all data & reports). Maintenance fee/Charges Is Rs. 99/- per month per store.
A Minimum 6 Month(s) fee need to recharge in a single recharge.
What Is The Maximum Resource I Can Use?
You can enter maximum 4000 new entry/voucher per month per store/branch. We store total 6 year data (2 year data in full permission mode & 4 year data in Read only mode).
If you need more resource, then contact us to know more about extra resource charges.
How Many Stores/Branches I Can Run In Saral Retail?
You can run One store/branche in Saral Retail. If you want more stores then you have to pay Rs. 19,999/- per store and add as many stores. Store add-on may take 1 working days.
How Many Users/Staffs In Saral Retail Application?
You can setup maximum 25 users / store in saral retail application.
Example if you have 3 stores/branches then, you can setup maximum 75 users. If one store has less then 25 users then, you can add more than 25 users in other store. You can change the user from one store to other store. you can allow any user to work in all store/branch.
If you need more users, then contact us to know more about extra charges.
What is taxation policies?
Taxes are extra, as per government of India policies.
